Infrastructure


Advanced manufacturing unit spread over an area of 36000 Sq. Mtr, having 4 Nos. manufacturing Bays equipped with specialized sophisticated machines .

Bay- 1 Aluminium Cryocans (1 Litre to 55 Litres capacity)
Bay -2 Small size Industrial Cryogenic Vessels (110 Litres to 1000 Litres capacity)
Bay-3 Medium and large size Cryogenic vessel ( 3000 Litre to 40000 Litres capacity)
Bay- 4 Large & Heavy pressure vessels,Lube equipments and Aviation equipments.

There are dedicated facility in above bays for manufacturing Carbon Steel and Stainless Steel separately.
